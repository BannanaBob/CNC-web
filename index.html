9000-DGI CNC
Intuitive yet Powerful

Intuitive yet Powerful At Milltronics, we are constantly refining our controls to simplify operation, shorten setup times, and provide features that reduce cycle times. The 9000-DGI Series control features 120 GB disk storage, 4GB memory, up to 10-times better graphic performance, mid-travel tactile keys, and an enlarged 15″ LCD touch screen. It’s a Windows®-based platform and offers all the user-friendly features that Milltronics CNC controls are known for, such as the G-code visualization screen.
